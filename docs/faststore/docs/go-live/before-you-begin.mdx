Before each production goes live, contact the FastStore team at least four weeks before the date and make sure that the following is complete:

<Steps>

### Account Edition

Your account edition is set to `vtex.edition-store@5.x` Edition app in your master workspace.

> ⚠️ If you do not have the appropriate Edition app installed, [open a support ticket](https://help.vtex.com/support) requesting the installation of the `vtex.edition-store@5.x Edition` app in your master workspace.

### FastStore Onboarding

The project was deployed during the [FastStore Onboarding](https://developers.vtex.com/docs/guides/faststore/getting-started-overview) process.  

### Storefront development

The storefront was developed during the [Setting Up the Project](https://developers.vtex.com/docs/guides/faststore/getting-started-2-setting-up-the-project).

</Steps>

## Next

Now, start integrating your FastStore project with VTEX by [Setting up a VTEX account for FastStore](https://developers.vtex.com/docs/guides/faststore/go-live-1-setting-up-vtex-account-for-faststore)
